Account & Registration

How do I create an account on Smart Campus Portal?

+

To create an account:

  1. Click on "Register" in the top navigation menu
  2. Fill out the registration form with your details
  3. Make sure to use your official college email address
  4. Choose a strong password following the requirements
  5. Verify your email address through the confirmation link sent to your inbox

Once verified, you can log in and access all portal features!

What should I do if I forgot my password?

+

If you've forgotten your password:

  1. Go to the login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password?" link
  3. Enter your registered email address
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the email within 10 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.

Can I change my personal information after registration?

+

Yes! You can update most of your personal information through your profile page:

  • Name and contact information
  • Academic details (year, department)
  • Interests and preferences
  • Profile picture

Some critical information like Student ID may require admin approval to change.

Events

How do I register for campus events?

+

Registering for events is simple:

  1. Browse events on the Events page or dashboard
  2. Click on the event you're interested in
  3. Review event details (date, time, venue, requirements)
  4. Click "Register for Event" button
  5. Confirm your registration
  6. You'll receive a confirmation email with event details

Make sure you're logged in to register for events!

Can I cancel my event registration?

+

Yes, you can cancel your registration:

  • Up to 24 hours before the event starts
  • Go to your dashboard and find "My Events"
  • Click "Cancel Registration" next to the event
  • Confirm your cancellation

Note: For paid events, refund policies may apply. Check event details for specific terms.

How do I get certificates for attended events?

+

Certificates are automatically generated after event completion:

  1. Attend the full duration of the event
  2. Mark your attendance through the portal or event coordinator
  3. Certificate will be available in your profile within 48 hours
  4. Download from "My Certificates" section in your dashboard

Certificates are digitally signed and can be verified through the portal.

What if an event gets cancelled?

+

In case of event cancellation:

  • All registered participants will be notified immediately via email and portal notifications
  • Full refunds will be processed for paid events within 5-7 business days
  • Event organizers may reschedule and give priority to already registered students
  • You can view cancellation status on the Events page

Technical Support

Which browsers are supported?

+

Smart Campus Portal works best on modern browsers:

  • Recommended: Chrome 90+, Firefox 85+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
  • Mobile: Chrome Mobile, Safari Mobile, Samsung Internet
  • Note: Internet Explorer is not supported

For the best experience, please keep your browser updated to the latest version.

The portal is loading slowly. What can I do?

+

To improve loading speed:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Disable browser extensions temporarily
  4. Try using an incognito/private browsing window
  5. Close other browser tabs consuming bandwidth

If the issue persists, contact our technical support team.

I'm not receiving email notifications. How do I fix this?

+

To ensure you receive notifications:

  • Check your spam/junk folder
  • Add noreply@smartcampus.ac.in to your contacts
  • Verify your email address in profile settings
  • Check notification preferences in your account settings
  • Ensure your email provider isn't blocking our domain

Contact support if you're still not receiving emails after following these steps.

Can I use the portal on my mobile device?

+

Yes! Smart Campus Portal is fully responsive and mobile-friendly:

  • Access all features on smartphones and tablets
  • Optimized for touch navigation
  • Fast loading on mobile networks
  • Full event registration and management capabilities

Simply open your mobile browser and visit the portal URL.

General

Who can access Smart Campus Portal?

+

The portal is available to:

  • Currently enrolled students at DEPSTAR
  • Faculty and staff members
  • Alumni (with limited access)
  • Guest users can browse public events but cannot register

Registration requires a valid college email address for verification.

Is my personal data secure?

+

We take data security seriously:

  • All data is encrypted during transmission and storage
  • We follow industry-standard security practices
  • Personal information is never shared with third parties
  • You can request data deletion at any time
  • Regular security audits ensure platform integrity

Read our Privacy Policy for detailed information about data handling.

How do I report a problem or bug?

+

To report issues:

  1. Use the "Contact Us" page to submit a detailed report
  2. Include screenshots if possible
  3. Describe steps to reproduce the problem
  4. Mention your browser and device information
  5. Our support team will respond within 24 hours

For urgent technical issues, call our support hotline: +91-2697-265012

Can I suggest new features for the portal?

+

Absolutely! We welcome feedback and suggestions:

  • Submit feature requests through the Contact form
  • Join our student feedback sessions held monthly
  • Email your ideas to feedback@smartcampus.ac.in
  • Popular suggestions are prioritized for development

Your input helps us improve the portal for everyone!
